Activity 3.1.2.

Determine which of the following are propositions (elementary or otherwise). If a given sentence is a proposition, determine its truth value. If it isn't, explain why not.

  1. Barack Obama was the 44th president of the United States.

  2. The square root of a whole number is always a whole number.

  3. The Green Bay Packers are the worst football team.

  4. Why is this class so much fun?

  5. This sentence is false.

  6. A group of crows is called a murder.

  7. Everyone likes cats.
