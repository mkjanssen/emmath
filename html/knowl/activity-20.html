Activity 5.3.8.

Suppose \(G\) is a graph with \(n\) vertices and that the vertex \(v_i\) has degree \(d_i\text{.}\) Explain why the following is true:

\begin{equation*} d_1 + d_2 + \cdots + d_n = 2\times (\text{the number of edges}) \end{equation*}

(It may help to draw some examples!)
