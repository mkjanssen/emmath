Axiom 2.2.1. (Carroll).

Consider the axioms:

  1. If a kitten loves fish, then it is teachable.
  2. Every kitten without a tail will play with a gorilla.
  3. All kittens with whiskers love fish.
  4. If a kitten has green eyes, then it is not teachable.
  5. All kittens have whiskers or do not have tails.
