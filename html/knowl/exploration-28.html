Exploration 5.2.23.

Let \(G\) be a planar graph with \(v\) vertices and \(e\) edges, and no loops or multiple edges.

  1. If \(v \le 6\text{,}\) why are we done?

  2. Now consider a graph with \(v = 7\text{.}\) Why must we have a vertex \(v_0\) of degree 5 or less?

  3. Temporarily remove \(v_0\) and any associated edges. How many colors are required for what remains? How many of those colors were used on the neighbors of \(v_0\text{?}\)

  4. Explain why there is a 6-coloring of \(G\) with \(v_0\) added back in.

  5. What if our graph has 8 vertices?

  6. What if our graph has 9? Or \(n\text{,}\) for any \(n\ge 1\text{?}\)
