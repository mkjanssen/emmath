Investigation 7.1.5.

Consider the following system. Each citizen of Scranton votes, and the votes are counted. The winner is the candidate with the smallest number of votes. This is known as minority rule.

  1. Suppose that the citizens of Scranton vote, with 76,327 voters voting for Stanley and one (Dwight, no longer a dictator) votes for Angela. Who wins under minority rule?

  2. Now suppose that Dwight convinces 38164 of the prospective Stanley voters to switch and vote for Angela. Who wins in this case under minority rule?

  3. Does minority rule treat all voters equally? Does it treat all candidates equally? Explain.

  4. Under minority rule, is it beneficial or detrimental for a candidate to receive additional votes? Explain.
