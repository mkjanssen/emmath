Activity 6.2.1.

Alice wants to send a bit of information to her friend Bob; for simplicity's sake, let's assume she seeks to send a 0 or 1. To ensure that the digit is received correctly, she decides to send it three times in a row. Bob will then read the intended digit as the digit that appears most often.

  1. How would Bob interpret Alice's message if he received \(111\text{?}\) What if he received \(010\text{?}\)

  2. What strengths and weaknesses do you see in Alice's system? How could you improve upon it?

  3. Perhaps you decided that Alice's system isn't sure enough, so you decide she should send the digit seven times. In what way(s) is this revised system better? Worse?
